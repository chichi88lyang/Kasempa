ABOUT US

Mission

To promote sustainable livelihood amongst vulnerable youths in Kasempa District and beyond through life changing skills, good health and other community based activities and reach out as many people as possible and make a difference in their lives.

Values

• Transparency
• Respect for human rights
• No Discriminatory
• Commitment
• Integrity
• No personal benefit
• Secular, Non-Political and Non-Religious
• See potential in every youth
All these values are upheld by all members of the organisation.

Vision

We envision an enabling environment for skills, talents and careers development. We believe that talent and skills are God’s gift and are a product of hard work and not birth. To fill the society with individuals who have the power of differentiating between what is good and bad for them as well as the society and its people living in one accord.

Our approach

Our Story

We reach out to youths and make them interact with other young people from Universities and other organizations and teach them different skills in poultry, metal and wood work, piggery and goat rearing. We also embrace them in a friendly way so as to provide them with a family environment for learning, make them understand our culture and hardships they need to overcome in the journey of their life. We also make them aware of the social issues they are in and how to make it a betterplace for them to survive and grow. A well defined smile is what we care for and try to achieve with our constant approach.
